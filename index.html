Short Bios

Prof. Zhou Su has research interests include Internet of Things, wireless communication and network security. He is the dean of the school of cyber science and engineering, Xi'an Jiaotong University. He has published more than 100 international journal and conference papers including IEEE/ACM ToN, IEEE JSAC、IEEE TMC、IEEE TMM、IEEETWC、IEEE TVT、IEEE TBD、IEEE TETC、IEEE TCSS、IEEE CM、IEEE NM、IEEE WCM, etc. He received the best paper award of IEEE ICC202, IEEE MSN2020,IEEE BigdataSE2019, IEEE Comsoc TCCGC 2018, IEEE CyberSciTech 2017, WiCon2016, and Funai Information Technology Award for Young Researchers in Japan. He is an Associate Editor of IEEE Internet of Things, IEEE Open Journal of Computer Soeity. He also served as the Co-Chair of several international conferences including IEEE/CIC ICCC2018, IEEE VTC Spring 2016, etc.
